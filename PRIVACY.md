# Privacy Policy — Odyssey

## 1. Data Collection & Use

Odyssey collects and processes limited data strictly necessary to operate and improve the bot’s functionality.

The following data may be stored:

* **Discord User ID**
  A unique identifier linked to your Discord account. Used to personalize your in-bot experience and manage user-specific features.

* **Channel ID**
  A unique identifier for a specific Discord channel. Used to associate bot activity and game data with the correct channel and ensure proper functionality.

* **Guild ID**
  A unique identifier for a Discord server. Used to manage server-specific settings, features, and stored data.

All collected data is non-sensitive and publicly accessible through Discord, and is used solely for operational purposes.

---

## 2. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide Odyssey’s services.

Data may be deleted when:

* It is no longer required for functionality
* A user, channel, or server is no longer using the bot
* The bot is removed from a server
* A deletion request is made

Users may request data deletion through the official support server by contacting the development team.

---

## 3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Odyssey does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

However, the bot may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party platforms.

---

## 5. Definitions

* **Odyssey**: The Discord bot, including all associated systems and services
* **Development Team**: Refers to the creators and operators of Odyssey ("we", "us", "our")
* **Discord Guild**: A Discord server or community
* **User**: Any individual interacting with Odyssey ("you", "your")

---

## 6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Odyssey after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
